Fueling the growth of early stage companies.

At Venture Veteran, we believe that entrepreneurship jumpstarts economic dynamism and unlocks growth potential in communities. Based on this, we look for big problems that affect either corporate organizations or individuals and think deeply about innovative approaches to solving them. When we come up with a solution that could potentially work, we then build a team, incorporate a company, raise seed funding and work with the team to build a sustainable and scalable business.
